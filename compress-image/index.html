Frequently Asked Questions

How do I compress an image online?

Upload your image to CompressNow, set your desired file size (e.g. 100KB), and download the compressed result. Everything processes in your browser — your image never leaves your device.

What's the best format for image compression?

For photos: JPEG or WebP. For logos and icons: PNG. For modern websites: WebP (25-35% smaller than JPEG). For iPhone photos: convert HEIC to JPEG first.

Can I compress images without losing quality?

Yes. PNG uses lossless compression — zero quality loss. For JPEG and WebP, our tool finds the optimal quality setting that hits your target size with minimal visible difference.

Is there a file size limit?

No hard limit. The tool works best with images up to 30MB. For larger files, try reducing dimensions first with our sister tool ResizeNow.

How does this compare to Photoshop or GIMP?

CompressNow is faster — no software to install, no learning curve. Upload, set target size, download. For simple compression tasks, it's much quicker than opening a full editor.
