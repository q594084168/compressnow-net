Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 14, 2026

1. No Uploads — 100% Browser-Based

All image compression happens entirely in your browser. Your images are never uploaded to any server. We do not have access to, store, or process your images on our servers.

2. Analytics

We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to understand how visitors use CompressNow. GA4 collects anonymous usage data including:

• Pages visited and time spent on site
• Browser type and device category (desktop/mobile/tablet)
• Approximate geographic location (country-level only)
• Referral source (how you found us)

GA4 does not collect your images, files, IP addresses (fully anonymized), or any personally identifiable information.

3. Cookies

GA4 uses first-party cookies to distinguish unique users. These cookies do not contain personal information. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

4. Cloudflare

CompressNow is hosted on Cloudflare Pages. Cloudflare may process your IP address and browser metadata for security and performance purposes. See Cloudflare's Privacy Policy.

5. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no personal data to access, correct, or delete. If you have questions, contact us via the website.

6. Changes

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted on this page.
